# Baseline Business Metrics

This section establishes the current baseline performance of the
food delivery platform prior to any optimization efforts.

## Key Metrics

- Order Conversion Rate: 50%
- Payment Success Rate: 67%
- Cancellation Rate: 20%
- Average Order Value (AOV): ₹542.85
- Delivery SLA Adherence (≤30 min): 25%

## Observations

- The order conversion rate indicates the overall efficiency of
  converting user intent into completed orders.
- Payment success rate highlights potential revenue leakage due
  to payment failures.
- Cancellation rate reflects order reliability and user trust.
- Delivery SLA adherence measures operational execution quality.

## Business Implication

These baseline metrics provide a reference point for identifying
high-impact improvement opportunities and for measuring the
success of future experiments and optimizations.
