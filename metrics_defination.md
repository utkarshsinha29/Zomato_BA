# Metrics Definition

## Primary Business KPIs

### 1. Order Conversion Rate
Delivered Orders / Menu Views

Measures end-to-end effectiveness of the platform.

### 2. Payment Success Rate
Successful Payments / Total Payment Attempts

Indicates revenue leakage due to payment failures.

### 3. Cancellation Rate
Cancelled Orders / Total Orders Placed

Tracks order reliability.

### 4. Average Order Value (AOV)
Total Revenue / Delivered Orders

Measures revenue efficiency.
