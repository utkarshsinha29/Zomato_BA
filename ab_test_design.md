## Objective
Increase order conversion by reducing uncertainty around delivery
time during checkout.

## Hypothesis
Displaying an accurate delivery ETA before payment will reduce
user anxiety and increase checkout-to-payment conversion.

## Experiment Design
- Control (A): Existing checkout without ETA visibility
- Variant (B): Checkout page showing delivery ETA before payment

## Target Users
All users reaching checkout stage.

## Metrics
**Primary Metric**
- Checkout-to-payment conversion rate

**Secondary Metrics**
- Order cancellation rate
- Delivery SLA adherence
- Average Order Value (AOV)

## Traffic Split
50% Control / 50% Variant

## Duration
14 days (full business cycle)

## Success Criteria
Statistically significant uplift in checkout-to-payment conversion
with no negative impact on cancellations or SLA.
